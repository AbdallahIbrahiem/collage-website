Faculty of Commerce

The Faculty of Commerce at Port Said University is a leading institution in business education and research. We offer undergraduate and graduate programs in a wide range of disciplines, including accounting, finance, management, marketing, and economics.

Our faculty members are experienced professionals and scholars who are dedicated to providing students with a comprehensive education in business and commerce. We also have strong ties with local businesses and organizations, providing our students with opportunities for internships, research collaborations, and career advancement.
