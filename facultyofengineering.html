Faculty of Engineering

The Faculty of Engineering at Port Said University is one of the most prestigious engineering faculties in Egypt. We offer undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields of engineering, including civil, mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering.

Our faculty members are highly qualified and experienced in their fields, and our students have access to state-of-the-art facilities and equipment. We also have strong ties with industry partners, providing our students with opportunities for internships, research collaborations, and job placement.
